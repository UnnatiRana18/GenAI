About Mitra

Mitra (meaning 'friend' in Hindi) is an AI designed to be a supportive listener. It's not a therapist, but a companion to help you navigate daily stresses by providing a safe outlet and suggesting simple, positive activities.

How It Works

1. Start a Chat: Click the button to open a new, private chat session.
2. Talk Freely: Share what's on your mind. Your conversation is anonymous and secure.
3. Find Support: Receive empathetic responses and gentle guidance.
